WASFF Grants

WASFF is the incorporated organisation which enables events such as Swancon.

Our charter allows for other projects which foster interest in science fiction within Western Australia.

If you have such a project but need some seed money to get it off the ground, we encourage you to apply for a WASFF grant.

If we award the grant, you will receive the money and access to WASFF's body of experience with running events, possibly including legal protection if your project is under the auspices of WASFF.

To find out more, you can email WASFF on wasff (at) sf.org.au, or talk to one of the current Board members.
